Let's plan something

From intimate dinners
to full-blown daawats

Whether it's a milestone birthday, a rehearsal dinner, a corporate night out, or a wedding that deserves real spice, Ma'am Saab brings the food, the cocktails, and the swagger. We cater on-site and off, host private events, and hand over the whole room for full restaurant buyouts in the heart of downtown Charleston.

You pick the occasion and the people; we'll bring the spice, the cocktails, and the kind of hospitality that makes a Tuesday feel like a celebration. Tell us what you're dreaming up, and we'll make it louder, warmer, and a little more delicious than you imagined.

Take the whole room

For a buyout, the entire restaurant is yours, every banquette, the low glow of intimate table lighting, the full bar, and a kitchen cooking only for your guests. Seated dinners for up to 50, standing receptions for 75. We'll build a family-style spread or a guided tasting around your night.

Our team handles the details, the playlist, the cocktails on arrival and the chai at the end. You handle the guest list and the toast. It's the most charming room in Charleston, and for one evening it answers only to you.

Or bring us to you

Weddings, corporate dinners, backyard daawats, we travel well. From drop-off to full-service with our team behind the chafing dishes, we bring Karachi to your venue and leave your guests talking for weeks.

Tell us the vibe and the headcount and we'll shape a menu to match, from passed chaat and kabab to a full sit-down feast with flavors designed to delight and satisfy.
